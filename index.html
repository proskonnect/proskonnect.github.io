How It Works

For Those Seeking Mentorship

  1. Sign up using our website or mobile app.
  2. Find a mentor that aligns with your needs.
  3. Set up a video call to meet your mentor.
  4. Continue engaging with your mentor via video calls, goals, and feedback.

For Potential Mentors

  1. Apply to be a mentor.
  2. Approve of potential mentees that have requested you as a mentor.
  3. Provide ongoing feedback and coaching.
